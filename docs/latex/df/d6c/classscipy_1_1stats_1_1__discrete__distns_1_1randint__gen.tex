Notes
-----

.. math::

    f(k) = \frac{1}{\texttt{high} - \texttt{low}}

for :math:`k \in \{\texttt{low}, \dots, \texttt{high} - 1\}`.

`randint` takes :math:`\texttt{low}` and :math:`\texttt{high}` as shape
parameters.
