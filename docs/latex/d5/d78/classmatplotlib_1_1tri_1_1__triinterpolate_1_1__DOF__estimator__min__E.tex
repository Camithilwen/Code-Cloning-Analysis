\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\begin{DoxyVerb}The 'smoothest' approximation, df is computed through global minimization
of the bending energy:
  E(f) = integral[(d2z/dx2 + d2z/dy2 + 2 d2z/dxdy)**2 dA]
\end{DoxyVerb}
