\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\begin{DoxyVerb}Pen calculating area, center of mass, variance and
standard-deviation, covariance and correlation, and slant,
of glyph shapes.

Note that if the glyph shape is self-intersecting, the values
are not correct (but well-defined). Moreover, area will be
negative if contour directions are clockwise.\end{DoxyVerb}
