\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\begin{DoxyVerb}Test behaviors of B-splines. Some of the values tested against were
returned as of SciPy 1.1.0 and are included for regression testing
purposes. Others (at integer points) are compared to theoretical
expressions (cf. Unser, Aldroubi, Eden, IEEE TSP 1993, Table 1).\end{DoxyVerb}
