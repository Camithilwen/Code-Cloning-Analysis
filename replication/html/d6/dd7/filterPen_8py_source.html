171 Shared by both DecomposingFilterPen and DecomposingFilterPointPen.
172

175
176 The 'skipMissingComponents' and 'reverseFlipped' optional arguments work the
177 same as in the DecomposingPen/DecomposingPointPen. Both are False by default.
178
